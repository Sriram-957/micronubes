About

We founded Micronubes Consulting to close the gap between ambitious data programs and the day-to-day decisions operators actually make.

Our story

After years inside analytics, data platform, and strategy roles, we kept seeing the same pattern: strong technology and talented people undermined by unclear goals, weak handoffs, and tools that did not match how teams work. Micronubes exists to fix that alignment — with crisp roadmaps, honest tradeoffs, and delivery your organization can sustain.

How we are different

  • We speak executive and practitioner: one narrative, two levels of detail.
